# ScreenSpace Content Policy

By uploading wallpapers to ScreenSpace, you agree to the following:

## Allowed Content

- Original video content you created
- Content you have explicit permission or license to share
- Nature, abstract, urban, cinematic, and artistic video wallpapers

## Prohibited Content

- NSFW, explicit, or adult content
- Hateful, violent, or discriminatory content
- Copyrighted content without proper license or permission
- Content that violates any applicable laws
- Spam, duplicate uploads, or low-quality content

## Moderation

- All uploads are reviewed by moderators before becoming public
- Content that violates this policy will be rejected or removed
- Repeat violators will have their accounts banned

## DMCA / Copyright Takedown

If you believe content on ScreenSpace infringes your copyright:

1. Email: dmca@screenspace.app
2. Include: your contact info, the content URL, proof of ownership, and a statement that you believe the use is not authorized
3. We will remove the content within 24 hours of receiving a valid notice
4. The uploader will be notified and may file a counter-notice

Repeat infringers will be permanently banned.

## Changes

This policy may be updated. Continued use of the upload feature constitutes acceptance of the current policy.

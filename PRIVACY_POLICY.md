# ScreenSpace Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-03-23

## What We Collect

When you create an account:
- Email address
- Password (stored as a bcrypt hash, never in plaintext)

When you use the service:
- Upload history (wallpapers you've uploaded)
- Download history (for counting purposes only)
- IP address (for rate limiting, not stored permanently)

## What We Don't Do

- No tracking or analytics
- No advertising
- No sharing data with third parties
- No cookies beyond session authentication

## Data Storage

- All data is stored on infrastructure we control
- Passwords are hashed with bcrypt
- API authentication uses JWT tokens stored in your macOS Keychain

## Your Rights

You can request at any time:
- **Export:** A copy of all data associated with your account
- **Deletion:** Complete removal of your account and all associated data, including uploaded wallpapers

To make a request, email: privacy@screenspace.app

## GDPR Compliance

For EU users:
- We process data based on your consent (account creation)
- You have the right to access, rectify, and delete your data
- You can withdraw consent at any time by deleting your account
- Data processing is limited to what is necessary for the service

## Self-Hosted Instances

If you self-host ScreenSpace, you are the data controller for your instance. This policy applies only to the official instance at api.screenspace.app.

## Changes

We will notify users of material changes to this policy via the app or email.
